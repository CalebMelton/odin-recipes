Description

These homemade (no canned meat) sloppy joes are simple to make and will take you back to simpler times. Add some chips or fries on the side and slop till you drop.

  • 1/2 lb Ground beef
  • 1/2 Medium yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 6 Spanish olives stuffed with minced pimientos, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add ground beef, cooking and stirring until browned and crumbly (10 minutes).
  3. Add onions and cook until soft (5 minutes).

  6. Roll empanada disks on a lightly floured surface until 1/2 inch larger in diameter.
  7. Spoon about 1 tbsp meat mixture into the center of each disk.
