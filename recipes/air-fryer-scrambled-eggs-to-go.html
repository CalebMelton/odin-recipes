Description

Follow this quick and easy recipe if you're running late for work and don't want to miss out on a hearty breakfast. You can add a couple sausage links next to the ramekin dish for some added protein. Just 10 minutes of prep and you'll be out the door and energized for the day before you know it!

Ingredients

  • Non-stick cooking spray
  • 2 Large eggs (beaten)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Lightly spray a ceramic ramekin dish with the cooking spray.
  2. Beat and pour in the eggs.
  3. Place the ramekin into the air fryer basket and set to 370° F (185° C).
  4. Wait 3 minutes.
  5. Stir egg mixture and wait 3 more minutes.
  6. Fluff with a fork and season with salt & pepper.
  7. Enjoy!
